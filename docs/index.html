Converta a maplumi tool
📍 Coordinate Cleaner

Turn messy coordinates into clean decimal degrees

Excel files love mixing longitude and latitude formats — DMS, degrees-minutes, decimal degrees, stray symbols. Converta normalises them all into clean decimal degrees, right in your browser. No install, no upload.

Understands your messy inputs:
  • Decimal degrees: 45.123N, -120.456, 120.456W
  • DMS & DM: 45°30'15"N, 120°30'W
  • Plain numbers and strings
Each row is flagged in Convert_Status — Yes, No, or Skipped.

Everything happens locally in your browser. Files never leave your device — ideal for a quick, private clean-up.
